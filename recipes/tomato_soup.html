Tomato Soup Recipe

Description

This Gluten-Free and Dairy-Free tomato soup recipe is an absolute staple in our home.

Ingredients

  • San Marzano Tomatoes
  • Fresh Basil
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Red Pepper Flakes

Steps

  1. Add olive oil to the pan.
  2. Carefully add tomatoes to the pan once oil is heated and shimmering.
  3. Add garlic.
  4. Bring to boil.
  5. Simmer on low for 40 minutes.
  6. Crush your tomatoes.
  7. Add basil and red pepper flakes to taste.
  8. Optional: serve with grilled cheese. Enjoy!
