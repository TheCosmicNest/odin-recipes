Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe

Description

This delightfully homemade Gluten-Free and Dairy-Free chicken tortilla soup recipe is a favorite for those looking for a lighter mexican style soup.

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Chicken Breast
  • Chicken Broth
  • Corn
  • Pinto Beans
  • Red Kidney Beans
  • Rotel

Steps

  1. Pan sear chicken breast.
  2. Set aside cooked chicken.
  3. Add chicken broth, corn, beans, and seasonings to pot to boil.
  4. Shred chicken.
  5. Add shredded chicken and rotel to pot.
  6. Bring to a boil.
  7. Simmer on medium-high heat for 45 minutes.
  8. Serve with tortilla chips, cheese, sour cream, and jalapenos. Enjoy!
