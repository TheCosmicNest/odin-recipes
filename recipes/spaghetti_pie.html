Spaghetti Pie Recipe

Description

This delicious Gluten-Free and Dairy-Free spaghetti pie is a family recipe, passed down from generations to generations, modified to accommodate food allergies.

Ingredients

  • Gluten-Free Spaghetti Noodles
  • Dairy-Free Half 'n Half
  • Dairy-Free Parmesan Cheese
  • Dairy-Free Mozzarella Cheese
  • Dairy-Free Cottage Cheese
  • Gluten-Free Spaghetti Sauce
  • Ground Italian Sausage

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil.
  2. Cook noodles until al dente. (Do NOT overcook noodles).
  3. In a pan cook your ground italian sausage until fully cooked.
  4. In a mixing bowl add all cheeses and season with salt and pepper while mixing. (Avoid over-mixing, you want this to be clumpy).
  5. Add spaghetti sauce to ground italian sausage.
  6. Add noodles to the mixing bowl with cheeses and add two eggs.
  7. Once eggs have been mixed in, add half of your pasta/cheese to a baking dish.
  8. Spoon half of sausage with spaghetti sauce on top of noodles/cheese in baking dish.
  9. Add the second half of noodles and top with remaining sauce.
  10. Top the entire thing with more shredded cheese if desired.
  11. Bake at 400 degrees for 40 minutes. Enjoy!
